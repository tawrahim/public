FAQ

How do I reset my password?

Enter your phone number on the login screen of the app and tap on the "Forgot my password?" text. We will send an instructions on how to reset it to the email that you signed up with.

Can I delete a trip after I create it?

We currently don't allow you to delete a trip after it has been created.

What happens if I forget to respond to my ride request?

tripBuddy is a very time sensitive app - what this means is that things are done in real time. If you create a trip and the time you set expires, tripBuddy would automatically notify all users that you did not respond to with a "declined" status.
